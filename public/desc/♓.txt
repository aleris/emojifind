A symbol representing the twelfth sign of the zodiac, often associated with duality, intuition, and creativity. Known for its connection to water and the mystical, it embodies empathy and dreamy energy.  

#  
- Representing the Pisces zodiac sign in astrology.  
- Symbolizing sensitivity and artistic expression.  
- Referencing the fish, as Pisces is depicted as two fish swimming in opposite directions.
