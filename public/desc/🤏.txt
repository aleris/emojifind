A hand gesture where the thumb and forefinger are pressed together, often indicating a small amount or precision. It can also suggest delicacy or careful measurement.  

#  
- Indicating something is tiny or minimal (e.g., "just a little bit").  
- Mimicking the act of holding or picking up something small.  
- Expressing precision or exactness in a task.  
- Signaling a subtle or nuanced point in conversation.
